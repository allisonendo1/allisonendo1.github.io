Tufts University - Student Marketing Consultant, The Tufts Career Center (Fall 2017)

I conducted a semester-long project with a team of five other students to improve the Tufts Career Center's marketing and outreach efforts. We began our market research by interviewing 80 current Tufts students to get suggestions on how The Career Center could improve their services and outreach methods. We also conducted comparative research to see what other services/outreach methods other schools utilize. We then used our comparative research to develop a comprehensive 3-year marketing plan that included suggestions on a variety of marketing tactics, such as blogging, email, events, social media, and branding.
